New South Wales

New South Wales is a state built with sprawling forests and stunning views!

Some recomended activities for you to try include:

  • New South Wales is well known for its variety of camp sites all around the state.
  • Visit some of the amazing beaches that the state has to offer such as Bondi Beach.
  • Visit some landmarks such as the Sydney Opera House where they have multiple shows running weekly
  • New South Wales is perfect for hiking. With amazing national parks such as the Blue Mountains National Park, you can't go wrong choosing to set your sights on New South Wales.
