Queensland

Queensland is the hot spot for tourist attractions in Australia!

Some recomended activities include:

  • Queensland isn't called the sunshine state for nothing. Here you can find the Gold Coast, the best beaches in the country.
  • There are a multitude of theme parks in Queensland which are a major tourist attraction for many people.
  • The Great Barrier Reef is a sprawling ecosystem just off the coast of Queensland that is 100 percent worth visiting.
  • Queensland also contains rainforests that can give you an interesting adventure to take part in.
